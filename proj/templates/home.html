    About Us Page

    Services: We are full integrated engineering company, it was established to provide architectural services according to the finest and most modern design principles. Our vision company offers unique luxury and elegance architectural designs. It contains a team of highly skilled and experienced architects, interior designers, landscape architects and construction managers. In addition to introducing the concepts of smart architecture and design using artificial intelligence and integrating them with architecture to make more effective, flexible and comfortable homes and buildings. The team works to show the aesthetic details of architecture, and tries to observe the details of the design in order to achieve the final result of the design in a correct, accurate, and error-free manner.

    ★☆★

    The first: engineering plans according to the principles of design and building controls, which are featured by the distribution of architectural spaces, achieving all spaces utilization and achieving the highest standards of sensory and visual beauty.

    ★☆★

    Second: Preparing complete two-dimensional architectural plans and converting them to three-dimensional with the distribution of furniture for each room, and the adoption of the interactive system methodology in the design that allows the owner to view the building in virtual reality during the design and understand the visual dimensions, architectural beauty, and the impact of the sun and the environment on the design.

    ★☆★

    The third: the Department of Interior Design and Furniture, where it works to solve all the architectural and interior design problems to create an interior field that serves the various functions of human life, and to employ all the fine and applied arts in an aesthetic and functional organization to create the appropriate internal environment.

    ★☆★

    Fourth: Exterior Design Department (Gardens), Choosing an exterior design that is suitable to the environment surrounding with consideration of the project location and the owner s budget. The exterior design includes both physical structures, in addition to technical designs, such as: paint color, roof shape, materials, floors, gardens, and so on.

    ★☆★

    Fifth: A complete inventory of the actual quantities according to the approved plans for the project.

    ★☆★

    Sixth: Incorporating intelligent, automated, programmable technologies into the building's HVAC systems to provide a dynamic HVAC indoor environment with the goal of achieving an optimal balance between occupant comfort and energy efficiency.

    ★☆★

    Sustainable architecture: a trend that seeks to preserve the natural environment and its resources and reduce environmental pollution resulting from buildings, through the use of natural building materials such as wood, stone, etc., and the use of clean renewable energies with efficient use.

    ★☆★

    Our vision: We aim to rely on high-performance smart buildings in terms of building systems and services such as comfort, heating, ventilation, air conditioning, lighting, security, safety and fire. And the promotion of the highest levels of control for the various standards that will determine the health of the environment inside the building. It is also equipped with smart generation systems using renewable energy sources through innovative technologies (storage and control), in addition to being compatible with future updates. Smart building not only enhances the productivity and well-being of users, but also helps achieve resource efficiency, cost-effectiveness, flexibility and adaptability.

    ★☆★

    Values: continuous development in the production. Work to ensure the rights of the engineer and the safety of the community. We embrace teamwork, integrity and dedication to make the business a success.
